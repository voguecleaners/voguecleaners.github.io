About Vogue Cleaners

At Vogue Cleaners, it is our objective to become a leader in our market by providing affordable, quality service and customer care. We use new products that are Eco-friendly, exceeding our customers' expectations. Don't worry, we still stick to some old(but good) methods as well. But most importantly we put quality and pride to everything we do, and we will.

Our specialties are wedding gowns, anything fabric from leather to silk, wash and fold, laundry, comforters, blankets, all sorts of alterations and more. We do not charge extra for one day/earlier service, because we understand how busy everyone is.

We will continuously improve all of our innovative processes to provide innovation that increases satisfaction, quality and value for our customers. We pride ourselves in maintaining our commitment to our customers and the community while providing a safe, enjoyable and environmentally responsible workplace for our employees.
